Research

My current research focuses on (provable) curriculum techniques for efficient and robust training of large language models. My works have spanned across different research directions, like neural tangent kernels, feature learning, implicit bias of optimization algorithms, skill localization in large language models, efficient pre-training, and test time adaptive architectures.
